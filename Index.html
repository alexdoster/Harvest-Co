HARVEST & CO SCHEDULING
Portfolio Demo

Harvest & Co is a specialty produce company that sends field chefs to grocery stores across the country to run live product demos. Coordinating dozens of chefs, hundreds of store locations, and multiple regional distribution centers requires more than a spreadsheet.

This app manages that operation end to end. Regional operators book demos and assign chefs to stores and products. Chefs log in from their phone to see their upcoming schedule, store addresses, and product info for each visit. Entries move through a status workflow — Scheduled, Confirmed, Completed, or Cancelled — so chefs always know what is locked in versus still pending.
